# WinSCP

**Using WinSCP FTP/SFTP Client to upload/download files from your Minecraft server** 

**1\)** Download [WinSCP](https://winscp.net/eng/download.php)

**2\)** Go to the your host's panel and click on your server's name

[![](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/483961286707773440/500956123604451329/firefox_2018-09-15_14-40-46.png?width=400&height=136)](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/483961286707773440/500956123604451329/firefox_2018-09-15_14-40-46.png)\*\*\*\*

**3\)** Go to the `Configuration` &gt; `SFTP Settings`[![](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/483961286707773440/500956334963818496/firefox_2018-09-15_16-41-23.png?width=400&height=198)](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/483961286707773440/500956334963818496/firefox_2018-09-15_16-41-23.png)\*\*\*\*

**4\)** On the next page **copy whole** Connection Address[![](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/483961286707773440/500956587637211136/firefox_2018-10-13_11-52-59.png?width=400&height=103)](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/483961286707773440/500956587637211136/firefox_2018-10-13_11-52-59.png)\*\*\*\*

**5\)** Open WinSCP and paste Connection Address to the Host name field

[![](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/483961286707773440/500956762258800640/WinSCP_2018-10-14_08-41-24.png?width=400&height=271)](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/483961286707773440/500956762258800640/WinSCP_2018-10-14_08-41-24.png)\*\*\*\*

**6\)** Enter username from Panel SFTP connection settings page and your panel account password

[![](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/483961286707773440/500957014982393866/WinSCP_2018-10-14_09-15-16.png?width=400&height=271)](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/483961286707773440/500957014982393866/WinSCP_2018-10-14_09-15-16.png)\*\*\*\*

**7\)** Go into Advanced Settings page

**8\)** Go to the `Connection` page then uncheck `Optimize connection buffer size` and click `OK` button

[![](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/483961286707773440/500957495179870208/WinSCP_2018-10-14_09-16-06.png?width=369&height=301)](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/483961286707773440/500957495179870208/WinSCP_2018-10-14_09-16-06.png)\*\*\*\*

**9\)** Click `Save`

\`\`[![](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/483961286707773440/500957571134521344/WinSCP_2018-10-14_09-16-40.png?width=400&height=271)](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/483961286707773440/500957571134521344/WinSCP_2018-10-14_09-16-40.png)\*\*\*\*

**10\)** Name Site, Check `Save Password` for future faster connecting and then click `OK`

\`\`[![](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/483961286707773440/500957832259043338/WinSCP_2018-10-14_09-17-29.png)](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/483961286707773440/500957832259043338/WinSCP_2018-10-14_09-17-29.png)\*\*\*\*

**11\)** Click on the `Login` button to connect to your server[![](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/483961286707773440/500957990745276416/WinSCP_2018-10-14_09-18-05.png?width=400&height=271)](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/483961286707773440/500957990745276416/WinSCP_2018-10-14_09-18-05.png)
