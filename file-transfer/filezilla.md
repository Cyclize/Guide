# FileZilla

**1\)** Download [FileZilla](https://filezilla-project.org/download.php?type=client)

**1\)** Go to the your host's panel and click on your server's name

[![](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/483961286825082890/500918239149424640/firefox_2018-09-15_14-40-46.png?width=400&height=136)](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/483961286825082890/500918239149424640/firefox_2018-09-15_14-40-46.png)\*\*\*\*

**2\)** Go to the `Configuration` &gt; `SFTP Settings`[![](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/483961286825082890/500918315217059841/firefox_2018-09-15_16-41-23.png?width=400&height=198)](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/483961286825082890/500918315217059841/firefox_2018-09-15_16-41-23.png)\*\*\*\*

**3\)** On next page you can see SFTP connection and login details you need to enter in your FileZilla Client Like from this example:[![](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/483961286825082890/500918549506949120/firefox_2018-09-15_16-47-52.png?width=400&height=78)](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/483961286825082890/500918549506949120/firefox_2018-09-15_16-47-52.png)\*\*\*\*

**4\)** To start, open FileZilla Client and click on the Sites manager button[![](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/483961286825082890/500918724820336641/unknown.png?width=346&height=300)](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/483961286825082890/500918724820336641/unknown.png)\*\*\*\*

**5\)** Click on the `New Site` Button

[![](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/483961286825082890/500918824648966164/unknown.png?width=375&height=301)](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/483961286825082890/500918824648966164/unknown.png)\*\*\*\*

**6\)** Give a New Site friendly name, maybe your Minecraft servers name for example

[![](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/483961286825082890/500919050277224473/unknown.png?width=375&height=301)](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/483961286825082890/500919050277224473/unknown.png)\*\*\*\*

**7\)** Change Protocol from FTP to SFTP[![](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/483961286825082890/500919222772301835/unknown.png?width=375&height=301)](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/483961286825082890/500919222772301835/unknown.png)\*\*\*\*

**8\)** Enter Host and Port from Panel SFTP connection settings page to the FileZilla Client, Change Logon Type From Anonymous to Normal[![](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/483961286825082890/500919646996660235/unknown.png?width=375&height=301)](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/483961286825082890/500919646996660235/unknown.png)\*\*\*\*

**9\)** Enter username from the Panel SFTP Connection settings page to the FileZilla Client and enter your panel password[![](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/483961286825082890/500919852790448129/unknown.png?width=375&height=301)](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/483961286825082890/500919852790448129/unknown.png)\*\*\*\*

**10\)** Connect!
